New paper announcement: matching fragments using images of fracture surface replicas

2022-05-01

I’m happy to announce a new paper in the Journal of Forensic Sciences, Quantitative matching of forensic evidence fragments utilizing 3D microscopy analysis of fracture surface replicas, which extends our prior work on fracture matching. Here we show that the method works when, instead of matching two fracture surfaces directly, we instead use replicas, which is common in forensic applications.
